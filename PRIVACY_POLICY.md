Privacy Policy – PeriMeno

Last updated: May 22, 2026

PeriMeno (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a symptom tracking app designed for perimenopause and menopause. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is stored and used.

Data collection and storage
- No account required: PeriMeno can be used without creating an account.
- No ads: The app does not include advertising.
- Local storage: Symptom entries and most app data are stored on the user’s device. The app is designed to be usable without sending your core tracking data to our servers.
- HealthKit (optional): If HealthKit integration is enabled, health data is accessed only with your explicit permission and is used to display insights in the app.

Purchases
Premium features (if purchased) are handled by Apple via StoreKit. We receive purchase tokens from Apple necessary to unlock premium access.

Sharing and reporting
The app can generate reports for your personal review and for discussion with your clinician. Generated reports may be exported/share using the standard iOS sharing mechanisms that you choose. We do not automatically share your data with others.

Your choices
- You can delete entries inside the app via history management features.
- If you have questions or need support, visit https://github.com/macsonn141-beep/PeriMeno.

Disclaimer
PeriMeno is intended for personal tracking and supporting discussions with clinicians. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or replace professional medical care.
